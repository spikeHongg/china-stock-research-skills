# Module Selection Cases

## Transition Company

- 从 `china-stock-research-orchestrator` 开始
- 通常会路由到 `strategy-business-transition`、`financial-health`、`risk-warning-catalysts`

## Project-Driven Engineering Company

- 从 `china-stock-research-orchestrator` 开始
- 通常会路由到 `business-decomposition-order-quality`、`financial-health`、`risk-warning-catalysts`

## Cyclical Materials Company

- 从 `china-stock-research-orchestrator` 开始
- 通常会路由到 `industry-competition-moat`、`valuation-investment-strategy`、`risk-warning-catalysts`

## Consumer Brand Company

- 从 `china-stock-research-orchestrator` 开始
- 通常会加载 `consumer-channel-brand` overlay，并路由到 `industry-competition-moat`、`business-decomposition-order-quality`、`risk-warning-catalysts`

## Regulated Healthcare Company

- 从 `china-stock-research-orchestrator` 开始
- 通常会加载 `regulated-healthcare-policy` overlay，并路由到 `industry-competition-moat`、`risk-warning-catalysts`、`valuation-investment-strategy`

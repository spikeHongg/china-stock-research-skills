# Moat Assessment Grid

Use these dimensions and support each with sources.

| Dimension | Questions |
| --- | --- |
| Cost advantage | Does scale, manufacturing, location, or financing lower cost? |
| Switching cost | Is the customer locked in by integration, certification, or migration pain? |
| Capacity or resource access | Is land, energy, quota, license, or supply hard to replicate? |
| Brand or qualification | Does formal qualification or reputation influence win rates? |
| Ecosystem position | Does the company sit where multiple parties depend on it? |

Label each dimension `Strong`, `Mixed`, or `Weak`, and explain why.

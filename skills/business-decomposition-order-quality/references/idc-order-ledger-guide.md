# IDC Order Ledger Guide

Use this guide for IDC, AI-compute, operator, and heavy-project names.

## Source Priority

| Source | What to Pull | URL |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Exchange announcement | Signed contract, project revision, related-party disclosure, risk note | https://www.cninfo.com.cn/new/index |
| China Mobile procurement | Bid winner, project package, procurement timeline | https://b2b.10086.cn/ |
| China Telecom procurement | Bid outcome, package scope, timeline | https://caigou.chinatelecom.com.cn/ |
| China Unicom procurement | Bid outcome, package scope, timeline | http://www.chinaunicombidding.cn/ |
| Local government / park site | Land, approval, power support, commencement, completion | Use project所在地 official website |

## What to Record

- Project name and customer
- Whether the company is equipment supplier, integrator, EPC contractor, investor, or operator
- Contract amount and term
- Whether the amount is confirmed, estimated, or framework-only
- Milestone and payment schedule
- Expected revenue-recognition basis
- Whether there is third-party public validation

## Key Distinctions

- A framework agreement is not equal to revenue backlog.
- A signed project is not equal to delivered revenue.
- Delivered revenue is not equal to collected cash.
- A ten-year operating term is not equal to ten years of stable utilization.

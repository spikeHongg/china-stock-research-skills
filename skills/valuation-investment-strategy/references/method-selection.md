# Valuation Method Selection

Choose the method that fits the business model and data quality.

| Method | Best For | Watch Out For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| PE | Stable profit businesses | Distorted by transition losses or one-offs |
| EV/EBITDA | Asset-heavy and infrastructure-like assets | EBITDA may ignore capex burden |
| PB | Asset-driven or cyclical balance-sheet stories | Book value may lag real economics |
| SOTP | Mixed business models or transition stories | Requires careful boundary setting |
| DCF | Businesses with visible cash ramp | False precision when cash flow is unstable |
| Asset-based | Real estate, data-center, resource assets | Requires realistic utilization and discount |

For transition names, start with SOTP or scenario-based EV/EBITDA plus a balance-sheet check.

# Sector Overlay Escalation Rules

Use sector overlays only after the generic pattern is clear.

## Escalation Principle

- Start from the generic pattern.
- Add a sector overlay only when sector-specific variables materially change at least two modules' evidence collection or judgment standard.

## Good Reasons to Escalate

- A regulated approval or reimbursement regime determines revenue timing.
- Channel inventory and same-store behavior determine whether reported growth is real.
- Commodity pricing, spread, or industry inventory dominate earnings.
- Export exposure, tariffs, FX, and customer concentration materially reshape profitability.
- Asset ramp-up, utilization, and financing structure dominate the thesis.

## Rule for IDC and Similar Examples

- IDC is an example overlay, not a default routing assumption.
- Load it only after the company already matches broader patterns such as `business-model transition`, `project or order-driven business`, or `asset-heavy ramp`.

## Output Reminder

State explicitly why each overlay was loaded and which modules it affects.

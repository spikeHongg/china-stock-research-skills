## High-Signal Indicators

- Major project revision or cancellation
- New order wins from operators or cloud-linked customers
- Ramp metric disclosure: occupancy, rack loading, power-on, or service-income start
- Operating cash flow deterioration despite delivery growth
- Short-term debt pressure or new financing announcement
- Policy or energy constraint affecting target region

## Good Threshold Examples

- Two reporting periods of negative operating cash flow during expansion
- Material increase in contract assets or receivables without matching collection disclosure
- Delay in project commissioning versus prior disclosed schedule
- Finance cost growth outpacing gross profit growth

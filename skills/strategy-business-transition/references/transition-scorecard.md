# Transition Scorecard

Use five dimensions for a transition score.

| Dimension | What to Check | Example Evidence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Capability | New technical, production, or operating capability | Annual report, project milestone, qualification |
| Customer mix | Old customers replaced by new target customers | Segment note, major contract announcement |
| Revenue quality | One-off delivery vs recurring or sticky revenue | Segment revenue note, contract term |
| Asset-light progress | Legacy assets shrinking or being impaired/disposed | Balance sheet, asset disposal notice |
| Cash conversion | New business turning into real cash | Cash-flow statement, working-capital note |

For each dimension, label `Strong`, `Mixed`, or `Weak` and attach at least one official source.

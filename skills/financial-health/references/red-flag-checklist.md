# Financial Red Flag Checklist

Flag these patterns early.

| Pattern | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- |
| Revenue up, operating cash flow down | Quality of earnings may be weak |
| Receivables and contract assets outgrow revenue | Delivery or collection quality may be deteriorating |
| Inventory surges without matching shipment | Demand or execution may be overstated |
| Finance cost rises while profit weakens | Leverage becomes more dangerous |
| Repeated impairment on legacy assets | Old business is still dragging the new story |
| Large short-term debt wall | Refinancing risk may dominate equity value |

For every red flag, cite the source and explain the path from accounting item to business risk.

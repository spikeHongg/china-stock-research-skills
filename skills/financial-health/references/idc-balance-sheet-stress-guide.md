# IDC Balance-Sheet Stress Guide

Use this guide when the company is building or buying heavy assets before full monetization.

## Source Priority

| Source | What to Validate | URL |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Annual and interim reports | Fixed assets, CIP, debt, receivables, contract assets, guarantees | https://www.cninfo.com.cn/new/index |
| Financing filings | Placement, convertible bond, bank loan, project-fund use | https://www.cninfo.com.cn/new/index |
| Exchange inquiries and replies | Accounting quality, project economics, impairment rationale | https://www.cninfo.com.cn/new/index |
| Official company disclosure portal | Supplemental investor materials | Use issuer official investor-relations page |

## Pressure Points

- Fixed assets and CIP rise faster than proof of operation income.
- Receivables and contract assets increase with project delivery claims.
- Inventory or equipment stock builds ahead of customer acceptance.
- Finance cost increases before recurring cash flow forms.
- Legacy-business impairments continue while new capex ramps.

## Questions to Force

- How much of the asset build is already producing cash flow?
- How much debt matures before the project reaches stable monetization?
- Does disclosed utilization or ramp-up justify the asset base?
- Is the company funding long-cycle assets with short-cycle liabilities?

# Regulated Healthcare and Policy Overlay

Use this overlay when reimbursement, centralized procurement, approvals, product registration, or medical-policy change materially affects valuation.

## Typical Signals

- Product approvals, tender outcomes, reimbursement, or pricing policy can re-rate the stock.
- Volume-based procurement, hospital access, or drug-device policy matters more than generic macro growth.
- Pipeline timing, regulatory review, and commercialization milestones are central.

## Questions to Force

- Which part of the equity story depends on policy rather than pure commercial execution?
- How vulnerable is price or margin to centralized procurement or reimbursement changes?
- What milestone must be approved before the next leg of growth becomes real?
- Which official regulator or healthcare-policy source confirms the key point?

## Output Reminder

Distinguish `policy optionality`, `approved fact`, `commercial adoption`, and `revenue realization`.

# Project and Order-Driven Business Overlay

Use this overlay when a company's earnings depend heavily on contract wins, backlog, milestone delivery, and customer acceptance.

## Typical Signals

- Large contract announcements are a major share-price driver.
- Revenue recognition depends on completion milestones or acceptance.
- Customer concentration and collection terms matter more than unit sales volume.

## Questions to Force

- Which orders are framework-only, which are signed, and which are actually executable?
- Which delivered revenue has turned into collected cash?
- Are contract assets, receivables, or inventory rising faster than credible execution progress?
- Which external source can validate the project, customer, or package scope?

## Main Sources

| Source | What It Validates | URL |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Exchange announcements | Signed contracts, revisions, cancellations, risk disclosures | https://www.cninfo.com.cn/new/index |
| Customer procurement portals | Bid wins, package scope, project timing | Use customer official procurement portal |
| Local government or official project websites | Commencement, approvals, completion, local support | Use relevant official website |

## Output Reminder

Keep four buckets separate: announced orders, executable backlog, recognized revenue, and collected cash.

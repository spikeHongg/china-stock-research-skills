# Business-Model Transition Overlay

Use this overlay when a listed company is shifting from an old business model to a new one.

## Typical Signals

- Legacy business still contributes assets, receivables, litigation, guarantees, or impairments.
- The company claims a new growth engine but disclosures show an overlap period.
- Capital allocation, organization, customer mix, and revenue mix are all changing at once.

## Questions to Force

- Is the new business changing revenue mix, gross profit mix, and cash-flow mix, or only the story?
- Is legacy drag shrinking fast enough?
- Does management fund the transition with equity, debt, or internally generated cash?
- Which milestone proves the new business is truly commercial, not just announced?

# Asset-Heavy Ramp Overlay

Use this overlay when fixed assets, construction in progress, utilization, and financing pressure dominate the investment case.

## Typical Signals

- Fixed assets, CIP, capex, and debt are rising quickly.
- The company is building capacity ahead of full monetization.
- Utilization, occupancy, load factor, or commissioning pace is more important than short-term accounting profit.

## Questions to Force

- How much of the asset base is already monetizing?
- Does disclosed utilization support the balance-sheet build?
- Is long-cycle asset build funded with short-cycle liabilities?
- What must happen operationally before the asset can earn a fair return?

## Output Reminder

Trace the path from capex -> asset build -> ramp-up -> cash generation -> equity value.

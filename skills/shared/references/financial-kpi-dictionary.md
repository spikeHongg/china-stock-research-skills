# Financial KPI Dictionary

Use this dictionary for A-share industrial, infrastructure, IDC, and heavy-asset transition cases.

## Quality of Growth

| KPI | Formula or Definition | What It Signals | Common Red Flag |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Revenue growth | Current period revenue vs prior comparable period | Scale expansion | Growth driven by low-quality one-off delivery |
| Gross margin | Gross profit / revenue | Pricing power and project quality | Fast revenue growth with falling margin |
| Operating cash flow / net profit | CFO / net profit | Earnings conversion | Sustained ratio below 1.0 |
| Contract liabilities vs revenue | Deferred revenue and prepayments | Upfront cash and order confidence | Revenue rises while contract liabilities weaken |

## Working Capital

| KPI | Formula or Definition | What It Signals | Common Red Flag |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Days sales outstanding | Accounts receivable / revenue x days | Collection efficiency | Rapid DSO expansion |
| Contract assets growth | Unbilled receivables | Revenue recognition quality | Contract assets outgrow revenue for multiple periods |
| Inventory turnover | Cost / average inventory | Sell-through and project execution | Inventory surges without matching delivery |
| Cash conversion cycle | DSO + inventory days - payable days | Working-capital pressure | Longer cycle during claimed business upgrade |

## Leverage and Solvency

| KPI | Formula or Definition | What It Signals | Common Red Flag |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Asset-liability ratio | Total liabilities / total assets | Balance-sheet leverage | Rising leverage with weak cash conversion |
| Short-term debt ratio | Short-term debt / interest-bearing debt | Refinancing pressure | Too much short debt during capex cycle |
| Interest coverage | EBIT / finance cost | Debt servicing ability | Coverage near or below 1.5x |
| Current ratio | Current assets / current liabilities | Near-term liquidity | Below 1.0 with large capex plan |

## Heavy-Asset Transition Metrics

| KPI | Formula or Definition | What It Signals | Common Red Flag |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fixed assets growth | Change in fixed assets and CIP | Asset-heavy transition pace | Fixed assets rise before monetization proof |
| Capex intensity | Capex / revenue | Investment burden | High capex without clear occupancy or load ramp |
| Utilization or loading rate | Industry-specific operating metric | Asset monetization | New assets remain underutilized too long |
| Impairment ratio | Impairment loss / equity or assets | Legacy-asset drag | Repeated impairment from old business |

## Output Rule

For every KPI used in a report, cite the underlying document and define the period and denominator.

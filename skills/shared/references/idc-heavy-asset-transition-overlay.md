# IDC Heavy-Asset Transition Overlay

Use this overlay when an A-share company is shifting from traditional engineering, manufacturing, or legacy infra work into IDC, AI-compute infrastructure, or data-center investment and operation.

This overlay does not replace the 6 core skills. It only adds sector-specific checkpoints.

## When to Load

- The company claims to be transitioning into IDC, AI-compute, or digital infrastructure.
- Revenue is being driven by large projects, equipment integration, or operator contracts.
- Fixed assets, construction in progress, debt, and utilization are central to the equity story.

## Sector-Specific Questions

### 1. Revenue Quality

- Is the company still mostly recognizing one-off construction or integration revenue?
- How much revenue is recurring operation revenue versus project delivery revenue?
- Does management describe a transition to operation income without showing matching ramp-up evidence?

### 2. Asset Conversion

- Are fixed assets and CIP rising ahead of monetization?
- Is the new asset base tied to self-operated capacity, leased capacity, or EPC-style pass-through work?
- Does the company disclose load-up, rack occupancy, power-on schedule, or customer onboarding milestones?

### 3. Counterparty Quality

- Are customers mainly operators, cloud vendors, local governments, or related parties?
- Can the contract be validated on an operator procurement portal or in an exchange announcement?
- Have there been revisions, delays, or partial terminations?

### 4. Energy and Location Constraints

- Is the project in a region aligned with public computing-infrastructure policy?
- Are power access, green-energy use, or PUE requirements mentioned in public documents?
- Is the local node likely constrained by land, power, or policy approvals?

### 5. Financing and Execution Pressure

- Is the transition financed by debt, convertibles, placements, or supplier credit?
- Do finance costs and working capital rise faster than the quality of recurring cash flow?
- Is the balance sheet carrying legacy-engineering risk at the same time as new IDC capex?

## Module Overlay Guide

- `strategy-business-transition`: test whether management has moved from EPC narrative to asset monetization proof.
- `business-decomposition-order-quality`: separate signed orders, delivered work, operating term, and cash collection.
- `financial-health`: focus on fixed assets, CIP, receivables, contract assets, debt tenor, and finance cost.
- `industry-competition-moat`: compare against operators, third-party IDC operators, cloud self-build, and integrators.
- `risk-warning-catalysts`: watch project changes, power access, funding progress, and ramp-up milestones.
- `valuation-investment-strategy`: prefer scenario-based methods that reflect asset loading and financing constraints.
